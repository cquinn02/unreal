The year is 2005, 1 year after the incident...

On the 27th of February of 2004 an unexplainable event occurs, the disappearance of a little boy with no evidence as to his whereabout or what occured to him. At first it is dismissed as a simple missing persons case but eventually more of such events start occuring. Forced to confront the reality of our world, secrets never dreamt of will be revealed.
